Need to be experienced?

Absolutley not, we gladly take both new and veteran divers, providing an unforgettable experience for even the most seasoned divers.

Are there restrictions on divers?

We can't take anyone under the age of 10 diving. Otherwise you should be good to go, we even take clients who don't know how to swim.

What sets us apart from our competitiors?

We focus on smaller and more personalized groups which lets new divers feel safer and gives experienced divers a chance to spread their wings without being held back.

Frequent Dive Sites

One of our most common dive sites is off the coast of Wakiki
